Global climate summit 2026: new commitments

2h ago By Kiu.Vibes staff

Leaders from 60 nations gathered in Nairobi to accelerate renewable energy targets. The summit concluded with a historic pledge to cut emissions by 45% before 2030. Additional funding of $200 billion was committed to green infrastructure projects in developing nations.

The agreement also includes a framework for carbon trading and technology transfer. Environmental groups have praised the outcome but urge swift implementation.
